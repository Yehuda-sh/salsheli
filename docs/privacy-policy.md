# Privacy Policy — MemoZap

**Last updated:** March 11, 2026

## Overview

MemoZap ("the App") is a household shopping list and pantry management app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data We Collect

### Account Data
- **Email address** — for authentication and account recovery
- **Display name** — shown to household members
- **Profile image** — optional, chosen by you

### Usage Data
- **Shopping lists** — items, quantities, prices you add
- **Pantry inventory** — products you track in your pantry
- **Receipts** — shopping summaries and costs
- **Household membership** — which household you belong to

### Technical Data
- **Device information** — OS version, app version (for crash reporting)
- **Firebase Analytics** — anonymous usage statistics

## How We Use Your Data

- **Core functionality** — managing lists, pantry, and household sharing
- **Notifications** — shopping updates, low stock alerts, household invites
- **Crash reporting** — via Firebase Crashlytics, to fix bugs
- **Analytics** — anonymous aggregate data to improve the app

## Data Sharing

- We **do not sell** your personal data
- Data is shared **only within your household** (members you invite)
- We use **Firebase (Google)** for storage, authentication, and analytics
- No third-party advertising networks have access to your data

## Data Storage

- All data is stored on **Firebase (Google Cloud)** servers
- Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- We retain your data as long as your account is active

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Access** your data through the app
- **Delete** your account and all associated data (Settings → Delete Account)
- **Export** your shopping history
- **Leave** a household at any time

## Children's Privacy

MemoZap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
